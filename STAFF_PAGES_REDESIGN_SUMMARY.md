## Design System

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Blue gradient (#667eea to #764ba2)
- **Success**: Green (#10b981)
- **Warning**: Orange (#f59e0b)
- **Error**: Red (#ef4444)
- **Background**: Light gray (#f8fafc)
- **Text**: Dark gray (#1e293b)

### Typography
- **Headers**: 28px, 24px, 20px (bold weights)
- **Body text**: 16px, 14px (medium weights)
- **Small text**: 12px (for labels and meta info)
- **Font family**: System fonts for better performance

### Components
- **Cards**: White background, 16px border-radius, subtle shadows
- **Buttons**: Gradient backgrounds, 10px border-radius, hover effects
- **Forms**: Clean inputs with focus states and validation
- **Modals**: Centered overlay with backdrop blur

### Spacing System
- **Small**: 8px, 12px
- **Medium**: 16px, 20px, 24px
- **Large**: 32px, 40px
- **Extra large**: 48px, 60px
